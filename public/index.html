Pixel Trading for Gaming Skins

IQSkins is a demo marketplace where players can cash out their Counter-Strike and Rust items with one click. We pay slightly below market value so that we can resell items later at a profit. Payouts arrive instantly via PayPal, card, or crypto.

How It Works

  1. Log in with your Steam account to load your inventory.
  2. Select the items you want to sell and confirm the trade.
  3. Receive your payout immediately. We then offer your skins on the marketplace.

Contact
